KENT Grand Star RO Water Purifier

Features

  • The multistage purification process makes water 100 percent pure and safe for consumption.
  • The smart RO purifier has a digital display of purity for the convenience of the users, the digital display feature informs the users about the filter life, RO flow rate, and purified water quality.
  • Kent grand star is India's first RO water purifier with an in-tank UV disinfection feature, this ensures that the water in the storage tank is purified and safe for consumption.
  • The water purifier has a high storage capacity which ensures continuous supply of water even in the absence of electricity.
  • Conventional RO purifiers reject almost 80 percent of the water and retain only 20 percent as purified.
