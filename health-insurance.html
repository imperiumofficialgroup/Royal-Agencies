Star Health Insurance

At Royal Agencies, we offer comprehensive health insurance policies through Star Health Insurance, providing peace of mind and financial security for you and your family.

Policy Options

We provide various health insurance plans, including:

  • Family Floater Plans: Covers the entire family under a single sum insured.
  • Individual Health Plans: Tailored for individual coverage with customizable features.
  • Critical Illness Plans: Offers coverage against life-threatening diseases.
  • Health Top-Up Plans: Additional coverage for existing health insurance policies.

Benefits of Star Health Insurance

Our policies come with numerous benefits, including:

  • Cashless hospitalization across a wide network of hospitals
  • Coverage for pre-existing conditions after a specified waiting period
  • Free health check-ups
  • No claim bonus for every claim-free year

For more information, you can visit the Star Health Insurance website.
