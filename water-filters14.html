Livpure Bolt Copper (RO + UV + Mineraliser) Water Purifier

Features

  • 7 Stage Purification: Livpure Bolt DX purifier provides 7 stage advanced purification: 1] Super Sediment Filter 2] Carbon Block Filter 3] Anti-scalant Cartridge 4] RO Membrane 5] Mineral Cartridge/ Mineraliser 6] Copper 29 Minerals Cartridge 7] Ultra Filtration Cartridge.
  • Smart TDS & Water Saving: Smart TDS technology enhances taste and provides purified & healthy water for input water TDS upto 1500 ppm. Water Saving technology helps save 20,000 litres of water every year.
  • Ultra Filtration and Anti-Scalant Technology: Ultra Filtration disinfects the water by removing bacteria and viruses without removing beneficial minerals, ensuring pure and safe water. Copper in the purified water boosts immunity, aids weight loss and improves digestion.
