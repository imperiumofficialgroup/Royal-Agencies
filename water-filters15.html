Livpure Zinger Copper (Hot RO + UV + UF + Taste Enhancer) Water Purifier

Features

  • Instant Hot Drinking Water with Water Saving: Enjoy instant access to hot, warm, and ambient water while conserving up to 20,000 litres of water annually. Smart TDS technology ensures great taste and healthy water, even with input water TDS levels up to 2000 ppm.
  • Multi-Stage Purification with Interactive LED Touch Display: Purification process involves multiple stages, including Super Sediment Filter, Carbon Block Filter, RO Membrane, Copper Filter, Ultra Filtration, Taste Enhancer, and In-tank UV Sterilization every hour. The interactive LED Touch Display offers convenience and clarity in operation.
