Aquaguard Ritz RO + UV Taste Adjuster Stainless Steel Copper Water Purifier

Features

  • Product Technology- RO + UV + MTDS with 8 stages of purification. RO Membrane removes contaminants like lead and mercury and eliminates disease-causing viruses and bacteria. UV e-boiling delivers water that is as safe and pure as water boiled for over 20 minutes. Taste Adjuster (MTDS) provides sweet tasting water by adjustment of taste depending upon the source of water.
  • Patented Active Copper + Zinc Booster Technology: The Active Copper + Zinc booster cartridge infuses the right amount of Copper and Zinc ions into the water. Copper and Zinc are known to boost the immune system and enhance the taste of water.
  • Patented Mineral Guard Technology- This helps to retain all the essential natural minerals such as Calcium and Magnesium, thereby providing the perfect blend of safe and healthy water.
  • Stainless Steel Tank- It is equipped with a storage tank made of high grade 304 stainless steel that is corrosion-free, tough and durable.
  • Water Suitability- This product is suitable for water from all sources (tanker/ borewell/ municipal water). This purifier can be used for TDS up to 2000 PPM (use a TDS meter to find out the correct TDS of your water source before buying a water purifier).
  • Convenient Features- a] High Storage Capacity, b] Smart LED Indication- Alerts you with service reminders, tank full indications, etc., c] Energy Saving Mode- Purified stored water can be dispensed without electricity. d] Supreme Aesthetics- Compact and stylish design that perfectly blends with your kitchen aesthetics, e] Flexible Installation- Wall-mounted/ Countertop.
  • Up To 60% Water Savings- Other ordinary water purifiers end up wasting 50-75% water, but Aquaguard water purifiers with superior technology purify water and regulate its taste to save up to 60% water.
